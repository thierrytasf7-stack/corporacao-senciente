# Generate Daily Briefing

## Purpose
Create comprehensive daily briefing consolidating information from all BET-SPORTS squads for strategic decision-making.

## Key Activities
- Collect daily reports from all squads
- Analyze performance metrics and trends
- Identify key issues and opportunities
- Generate actionable insights
- Prepare briefing for strategic review

## Inputs
- Daily reports from live-betting, data-sports, strategy-sports, infra-sports, and analytics-sports squads
- Performance metrics and KPIs
- Risk exposure reports
- Market conditions and trends

## Outputs
- Consolidated daily briefing report
- Key performance indicators summary
- Risk assessment and exposure report
- Strategic recommendations

## Success Metrics
- Completeness of information coverage
- Accuracy of analysis and insights
- Actionability of recommendations
- Timeliness of briefing delivery
