# Orchestrate Performance Analytics

## Purpose
Coordinate the analytics-sports squad to analyze betting performance and generate insights for strategic decisions.

## Key Activities
- Analyze betting performance across all squads
- Generate comprehensive performance reports
- Identify trends and patterns in betting data
- Provide insights for strategy optimization
- Monitor portfolio performance and risk exposure

## Inputs
- Betting performance data
- Risk exposure metrics
- Market performance analysis
- Strategy effectiveness reports

## Outputs
- Performance analysis reports
- Risk exposure assessments
- Strategic insights and recommendations
- Portfolio optimization proposals

## Success Metrics
- Accuracy of performance analysis
- Quality of strategic insights
- Impact on decision-making
- Portfolio performance improvements
