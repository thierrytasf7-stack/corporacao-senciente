# Orchestrate Live Betting Operations

## Purpose
Coordinate the live-betting squad to execute real-time betting operations, manage risk, and optimize performance during live sports events.

## Key Activities
- Monitor live sports events and betting opportunities
- Coordinate instant betting decisions
- Manage bankroll allocation for live bets
- Monitor risk exposure and adjust strategies
- Generate real-time performance reports

## Inputs
- Live sports event data
- Current betting odds and market conditions
- Bankroll status and risk limits
- Performance metrics from previous bets

## Outputs
- Coordinated betting decisions
- Risk-adjusted stake recommendations
- Real-time performance reports
- Strategy adjustments based on market conditions

## Success Metrics
- Win rate during live betting
- Risk-adjusted returns
- Response time to betting opportunities
- Adherence to risk management protocols
