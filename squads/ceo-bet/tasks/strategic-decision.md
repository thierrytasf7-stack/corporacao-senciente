# Make Strategic Decisions

## Purpose
Make high-level strategic decisions to optimize betting portfolio performance and guide squad operations.

## Key Activities
- Review comprehensive data and analysis
- Evaluate strategic options and scenarios
- Make data-driven decisions
- Communicate decisions to squads
- Monitor decision impact and adjust as needed

## Inputs
- Daily briefing and performance reports
- Risk assessment and exposure analysis
- Market trends and opportunities
- Squad performance and capabilities

## Outputs
- Strategic decisions and action plans
- Resource allocation recommendations
- Risk management adjustments
- Performance targets and goals

## Success Metrics
- Quality and impact of strategic decisions
- Achievement of performance targets
- Risk management effectiveness
- Portfolio optimization results
