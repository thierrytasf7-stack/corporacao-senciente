# Strategic Planning Task

## Purpose
Develop a comprehensive strategic plan to achieve a high-level goal.

## Inputs
- **Goal:** The primary objective to be achieved.
- **Context:** Current state, constraints, and available resources.
- **Timeline:** Desired timeframe for completion.

## Outputs
- **Strategic Plan Document:** A detailed document outlining the strategy.
- **Key Milestones:** High-level milestones to track progress.
- **Risk Assessment:** Identification of potential risks and mitigation strategies.

## Steps
1.  **Analyze the Goal:**
    - Decompose the high-level goal into specific objectives.
    - Identify key success indicators (KPIs).

2.  **Assess Context:**
    - Evaluate current capabilities and resources.
    - Identify external factors and constraints.

3.  **Develop Strategy:**
    - Define the approach to achieve each objective.
    - Select appropriate methodologies and technologies.

4.  **Plan Execution:**
    - Define high-level milestones and deliverables.
    - Estimate resource requirements.

5.  **Risk Management:**
    - Identify potential risks.
    - Develop mitigation plans.

6.  **Document:**
    - Compile all findings into a structured Strategic Plan document.
