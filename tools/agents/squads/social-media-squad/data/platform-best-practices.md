# Platform-Specific Guidance

## Instagram
- **Visual storytelling**: Carousel posts for narrative progression, single images for impact
- **Reels optimization**: 7-15 second ideal length, trending audio usage, text overlays for sound-off viewing
- **Timing strategy**: Peak engagement 11am-2pm and 7-9pm in audience timezone
- **Hashtag strategy**: Mix of 10-15 hashtags (3 broad, 5 niche, 7 branded/community)
- **Story engagement**: Polls, questions, and sliders to boost algorithmic favor

## TikTok
- **Trend participation**: Jump on trends within 24-48 hours of emergence
- **Native video**: Shoot within app when possible for authentic feel
- **Speed principles**: Hook in first 3 seconds, deliver value by 10 seconds
- **Authenticity focus**: Behind-the-scenes, unfiltered moments outperform polished content
- **Sound strategy**: Original audio for brand recognition, trending sounds for reach

## LinkedIn
- **Professional insights**: Share industry analysis, career lessons, business strategies
- **Thought leadership**: Position brand/person as expert through consistent, valuable content
- **Engagement tactics**: Ask questions, tag relevant people, respond to every comment
- **Content mix**: 70% insights, 20% company news, 10% personal stories
- **Timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am for maximum professional engagement

## Twitter/X
- **Conversation focus**: Reply to industry conversations, participate in Twitter Spaces
- **Hot takes**: Share timely opinions on industry news (controversy drives engagement)
- **Community building**: Create and nurture Twitter Lists, engage with mentions daily
- **Thread strategy**: 5-7 tweet threads for complex topics, hook tweet with strong first line
- **Visual integration**: Images increase engagement by 150%, videos by 200%

## YouTube
- **Long-form value**: 8-15 minute videos for educational content, 20-30 for deep dives
- **Education focus**: How-to content, tutorials, explainers perform best
- **Personality showcase**: Let brand voice shine through - YouTube rewards connection
- **SEO optimization**: Keyword-rich titles, descriptions, and tags for discoverability
- **Community engagement**: End screens, cards, and pinned comments to drive interaction
